You're planning a week in Lisbon. Airbnb apartment for $100 a night or hotel for $145? Sounds like a simple calculation. It isn't.

The choice between Airbnb and hotel depends on factors that have little to do with price. Plus: there's a third path most travelers overlook.

## When does Airbnb actually make sense?

Airbnb is the right choice in four situations.

Long stays. After 5-7 nights, Airbnb usually becomes cheaper. You skip daily restaurants, can cook, do laundry. For a month in a city, the savings are dramatic.

Groups of 4 or more. Four people need two hotel rooms. A 3-bedroom Airbnb is usually cheaper than two rooms and you stay together.

Local experience. If you want to live in a typical neighborhood instead of the hotel district, Airbnb gives you broader choice. In Brooklyn, the Marais, Vila Madalena.

Cooking instead of restaurants. If dietary or financial reasons require cooking, you need a kitchen. Most hotels don't have that.

## When is a hotel the better choice?

Hotels have advantages Airbnb can't catch up on.

Short stays. 1-3 nights are usually less stressful in a hotel. No key handover appointment, no house rule discussion, classic 24-hour reception.

Guaranteed cleanliness. Hotels have staff that cleans daily and maintains minimum standards. Airbnb cleaning quality varies wildly, from spotless to scary.

Reliability. Hotels almost never cancel reservations. Airbnb hosts do. A 2024 [Better Business Bureau](https://www.bbb.org/) overview noted that around 7 percent of all Airbnb bookings get cancelled by hosts at short notice.

Service. Concierge, 24h reception, breakfast, luggage storage after checkout. Small things that make a big difference when traveling.

## What many travelers overlook: Booking.com has both

Here's the third path most travelers don't realize exists.

Booking.com has long stopped being just hotels. Since 2018, the platform has aggressively added apartments, vacation rentals and private accommodations. Today on Booking.com you find:

- Classic hotels
- Boutique hotels
- Apartments and studios
- Vacation homes
- B&Bs and guesthouses

A concrete example: a typical Airbnb apartment in Lisbon's Alfama district is very often also listed on Booking.com. Same provider, same apartment, often the same photos. But different booking conditions.

Industry estimates: about 40 to 50 percent of all Airbnb apartments in European cities are listed in parallel on Booking.com. Owners want maximum visibility and use both platforms.

What Booking.com does better for apartment bookings.

**Cancellation protection.** Booking.com almost always offers a free-cancellation option. Airbnb is significantly stricter here. With many Airbnb bookings you lose 50 to 100 percent on cancellation.

**Clear pricing.** With Airbnb, cleaning fees and service charges often only appear at checkout. A $100 night quickly becomes $148. On Booking.com you see the final price upfront.

**Customer service.** Booking.com has a 24/7 hotline with phone number in your language. Airbnb support is notorious for long response times and chatbots.

**Reviews.** Booking reviews come only from guests who actually stayed. The 1-10 scale is more objective than Airbnb's 5-star system.

## How do you find the best deal?

A pragmatic search strategy for every trip.

**Step 1:** Search for your dream apartment on Airbnb. Find 3-5 options you like. Save photos and address.

**Step 2:** Search for the same apartments on Booking.com. Type the neighborhood plus apartment type into the search field, scroll through listings. You recognize the same apartments often by photo or description style.

**Step 3:** Compare. Booking is often cheaper or has better cancellation. With equal pricing: always go with Booking for the service.

**Step 4:** If the apartment exists only on Airbnb, read reviews very carefully. Avoid hosts with under 4.8 stars or fewer than 30 reviews.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is cheaper: Airbnb or hotel?

For short stays (1-3 nights) hotels are often cheaper or the same, especially with breakfast included. From 5+ nights or groups of 4+ people, Airbnb wins clearly.

### When should I use Booking.com instead of Airbnb?

Whenever the same apartment is listed on both. Booking has better cancellation protection, clearer final price and 24/7 service. About 40 to 50 percent of all Airbnb apartments are also on Booking.

### Which review platform is more reliable?

Booking.com is more objective because only actual overnight guests can review. The 1-10 scale runs stricter than Airbnb stars. For hotels look for 8+ rating, for apartments 9+.

### How do you avoid Airbnb booking cancellations?

Choose hosts with "Superhost" status, at least 50 reviews, response rate above 90 percent. Don't book too close to the trip, since hosts often cancel when they get better last-minute bookings.
