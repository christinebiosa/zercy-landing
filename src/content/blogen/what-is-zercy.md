Travel planning has never been more accessible, yet somehow more exhausting. Skyscanner in one tab, Booking.com in another, rental cars in a third, Google Maps in a fourth. Every platform shows a fragment. None of them thinks the whole picture.

Zercy does.

## How has AI changed travel planning?

The old travel agency had one strength no app has ever replaced: it listened. A travel advisor knew your budget, your style, your preferences, and put everything together for you. Flight, hotel, excursions. Done.

Then came the internet. With it: access to everything. Thousands of flights, millions of hotels, hundreds of comparison sites. What got lost in the process was the thinking. Platforms calculate. They don't understand.

AI brings that thinking back. Zercy combines the digital access of today with the personal intelligence of the past. The best of both worlds: free, instant, no sign-up needed.

## What can Zercy plan for you?

Zercy thinks your trip holistically, not as isolated components, but as a connected experience. What you type, Zercy understands like a human. What it gives back is a complete plan.

Here's what Zercy covers:

- **Flights:** direct routes, stopover options, optimal booking windows, multi-airport comparisons
- **Hotels & accommodation:** from design hotels to apartments, tailored to your budget and travel style
- **Rental cars:** with real prices, pickup location, insurance tips and drop-off options
- **Trains & buses:** Europe's rail network, intercity connections, night trains, regional alternatives
- **Tours & activities:** local experiences, city tours, day trips, bookable directly
- **Navigation:** Google Maps is integrated, travel times from airport to hotel are calculated automatically

If you're heading to [Santiago de Compostela](/en/blog/santiago-de-compostela) and want to continue to Porto, Zercy gives you the train, the travel time and the right departure schedule, without you having to search for it separately. That's AI that genuinely thinks ahead.

## Why is booking through Zercy safe?

Trust is everything when it comes to booking travel. That's why Zercy has one clear principle: your payment always runs through the official websites of the providers. Zercy never sees your payment details. Zercy never stores them.

You pay with the payment methods you already use:

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit card
- PayPal
- Klarna, depending on the provider

No fake bookings. No fraud. No middleman taking a cut. Your booking is exactly as secure as going directly to Booking.com or Skyscanner, because that's exactly what you're doing. Zercy is the assistant that shows you the way. You open the door yourself.

## How does Zercy work in practice?

Just type your travel idea. The way you'd tell a friend.

Zercy is completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees. Zercy earns through partner links to booking platforms. Nothing changes for you at all.
