## What Is the Zercy Logbook?

You have booked. Flight, hotel, rental car. Everything reserved, everything confirmed. And then? Your confirmations are scattered across your inbox. On three different platforms. In two PDFs. Somewhere in a screenshot on your phone.

That is annoying. And that is exactly what the Zercy Logbook solves.

It brings everything together: a clean, color-coded timeline for every trip you make. AI-powered, automatically built, always at hand. No subscription. No download. Just log in and go.

## Three Ways to Add Bookings

What makes the Zercy Logbook special is its flexibility. There is no single right way. There are three. And all three are processed by AI in the background.

- **Paste your booking confirmation:** Copy the text from your confirmation email and paste it directly into the Logbook. Booking.com, Lufthansa, Rentalcars, Airbnb: it doesn't matter where it came from. The AI reads everything, recognizes flight numbers, dates, prices and booking codes, and builds a structured entry. Nothing needs to be filled in manually. But you can always edit any entry by hand afterwards.
- **Forward by email:** Even easier. Forward your booking email to plans@in.zercy.app. That's it. The Logbook receives the email automatically, the AI reads it, and the entry appears in your timeline. No copying, no opening an app, no extra step. The confirmation lands in your inbox, the trip lands in your Logbook.
- **Type it informally:** Sometimes you don't have a confirmation at hand. No problem. Just type what you know: "Rental car from Frankfurt to Cologne on the same day as hotel check-in." The AI understands the context. It knows your other bookings in the trip and resolves relative date references automatically. Works in English, German, or mixed. Short notes are enough. And you can always add or change something afterwards. Totally flexible.

## The Timeline: This Is What Your Trip Looks Like

Every booking lands as a color-coded card on the timeline. Flights appear in orange. Hotels in blue. Rental cars in green. Trains in purple. At a glance, you see what happens when.

The entries are detailed. A flight shows the flight number, departure and arrival times, seat and baggage info. Hotels appear as two entries: check-in and check-out, each with times. Layovers between flight legs are added automatically, including the exact waiting time.

None of this needs to be typed in manually. The AI builds the entire structure. You just read it.

## Login Without a Password

The Zercy Logbook does not need a password. You enter your email address and receive a magic link. One click and you are in. The login stays valid for 90 days, on your phone, your laptop, and everywhere else.

Logging out does not delete any data. All your trips and bookings stay saved. The next time you log in via magic link, everything is right where you left it.
