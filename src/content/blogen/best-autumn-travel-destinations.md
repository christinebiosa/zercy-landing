Autumn is the most underrated travel season. The crowds have left. Prices drop. Light turns warmer. Some destinations only show their real face when the high-summer rush is over.

Travelers who go in September, October, or November often get hotels and flights 20-40 percent cheaper than in August. At some destinations even more. At the same time, the weather in many regions is still excellent, or only becomes truly good in autumn.

## Which destinations are at their best in September and October?

**Tuscany (Italy):** The grape harvest happens September through October. Rows of vines in golden tones, cypress trees, Chianti hills. Fewer tourists than in August. Agriturismo prices fall 20-35 percent. This is the Tuscany of the postcards, but actually enjoyable.

**Greece (Crete, Rhodes):** October is the secret month. Sea temperature 72-75°F (still warm enough to swim). Hotels often 40-50 percent cheaper than August. Attractions without queuing. Many resorts don't close until late October.

**Portugal (Alentejo, Douro Valley):** September and October are the best travel time. Wine harvest, golden light, temperatures 72-82°F. The Douro Valley in autumn is one of the most beautiful travel destinations in Europe. [Visit Portugal](https://www.visitportugal.com/en) has information on wine festivals.

**Morocco:** October is high season in Morocco. Extreme summer heat gone, Atlantic water temperatures still pleasant. Marrakech in October is dramatically more bearable than July or August.

## Why is November an underrated travel month?

November is the best month for specific destinations that are unsuitable in summer.

**Madeira:** November on Madeira is a quiet insider tip. Temperatures 68-73°F, no peak season crowds, hiking trails clear, levadas green. Cheaper flights from most of Europe than in summer months. The island works year-round — but November is especially calm.

**Vietnam (Hoi An, Da Nang):** November is transition time. Southern Vietnam is dry. Prices affordable. Tourist season only starts in December. Those who go in November have things largely to themselves.

**Canary Islands:** The Canaries work year-round, but November is special: 72-75°F, no heat waves, Europeans not yet arriving in numbers. For hikers, Tenerife in November is ideal.

**Japan in autumn (late October to mid-November):** Koyō, the Japanese autumn foliage, is one of the most spectacular natural phenomena in the world. Kyoto and Nikko in this period: unparalleled. But also: expensive and crowded. Early booking is essential.

## What are the cheapest autumn travel destinations in Europe?

**Albania:** October and November are the best months. Pleasant weather, no crowds, minimal prices. Albania's Riviera empties after September. The country is [recognized as a budget destination](https://albania.travel/en) while remaining completely authentic.

**Romania (Transylvania):** October with autumn foliage in the Carpathians, Dracula season (touristy but fun), Brașov and Sibiu without peak-season prices. Hotel nights in excellent properties from $48-65.

**Poland (Kraków, Warsaw):** Cooler in October but Kraków is stunning in autumn. Jewish history, medieval old town, Wawel Castle. Prices below Western European comparable cities.

## When should you book autumn trips for the best price?

Autumn bookings work differently than summer.

- **Last-minute (2-4 weeks ahead):** Viable for non-peak destinations. Hotels have availability, prefer filling at lower rates over empty.
- **Early booking (6+ months out):** Essential for Japan autumn foliage, popular Italian routes, Tuscan agriturismos.
- **Set price alerts:** Skyscanner and Google Flights allow price alerts. Set them for autumn destinations in July, book when prices drop.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which European destinations still have good swimming weather in October?

Crete, Rhodes, and Cyprus have sea temperatures of 72-75°F in October. The Canary Islands year-round at 64-72°F. Southern Spain (Andalusia, Costa del Sol) still pleasantly warm in October. Everything north of that: too cold to swim.

### Why are prices cheaper in autumn?

Peak season in Europe is July/August (school holidays, high summer). After that, demand drops sharply. Hotels and airlines respond with lower prices. Exceptions: carnival destinations, wine harvest festivals, Japan's koyō season where demand remains high.

### Which autumn destinations work best for hiking?

Alps: September-October ideal (mountain huts still open, stable weather). Carpathians in Romania and Czech Republic: October with autumn colors. Camino de Santiago: September is the most popular time. Iceland: still hikeable in October, but shorter days and changeable weather.

### How does autumn travel differ for solo travelers vs. couples vs. families?

Solo travelers benefit most from autumn: single room prices drop sharply, crowds are gone, easier to connect with other travelers in shoulder season. Couples: ideal for romantic European cities in autumn light. Families: constrained by school schedules, use the October half-term break.
