Christchurch reinvented itself. The 2011 earthquake destroyed parts of the inner city. What emerged afterward is one of the most interesting urban transformations in the world. Container malls, new art installations, innovative architecture where office buildings once stood. Christchurch today is unlike any other New Zealand city. More modern, more open, more unexpected.

For travelers it is the best gateway to the South Island. The choice of neighborhood depends on whether you want to experience the new city or live quietly outside it.

## Which Neighborhood Fits Your Trip?

**City Centre / Avon River:** The new city center with Botanic Gardens and the Avon River promenade. For first-timers wanting to experience the rebuilt city.

**Riccarton:** West of the center, large shopping mall, lots of hotels, practical location. For budget travelers and shoppers.

**Fendalton / Merivale:** Upscale residential suburbs north of the center. For travelers wanting quiet accommodation with good restaurants.

**Sumner:** Coastal and cliff suburb east of the city. For nature lovers wanting coastal atmosphere.

**New Brighton:** Budget beach area east, pier, less tourism. For budget travelers with a beach preference.

## City Centre and Avon River: What Does It Cost to Stay in Christchurch?

The new city center around the Avon River is one of the world's most interesting post-earthquake urban spaces. Re:START Mall (container city), Margaret Mahy Playground, Christchurch Art Gallery, the new cathedral (still under construction in 2026). Hotels here are mostly new builds. Fresh, modern, well-equipped.

**Who belongs here:** First-timers, culture lovers, anyone wanting to be near the Avon promenade and Botanic Gardens.

**Prices per night:** Budget hostels 30-60 NZD (17-33 EUR), Midrange 120-220 NZD (66-121 EUR), Premium 220-400 NZD (121-220 EUR).

**Top picks:** The Piano Hotel (boutique hotel in the cultural center, very popular), Crowne Plaza Christchurch (reliable premium hotel with central location), Rydges Latimer Christchurch (solid upscale midrange, good value). Find over 130 hotels in Christchurch on [Booking.com](https://www.booking.com).

The official [Christchurch and Canterbury Tourism](https://www.christchurchnz.com) has current information on rebuild highlights, events, and activities in the Canterbury region.

## Riccarton: Practical and Well Connected

Riccarton sits on Riccarton Road, the main shopping street, west of the center. Westfield Riccarton is the region's largest shopping mall. Hotels are solid, affordable, and well connected by bus lines. No great atmosphere. But perfect for transit travelers.

**Who belongs here:** Budget travelers, families, those prioritizing function over atmosphere.

**Prices:** Hostels 25-50 NZD, Budget midrange 80-160 NZD (44-88 EUR).

**Top picks:** Ibis Christchurch (most affordable reliable chain hotel in the area), Sudima Christchurch City (good midrange, sustainability certification), Christchurch YHA (best hostel in a central location).

## Fendalton and Merivale: When Is the Best Time to Visit Christchurch?

Fendalton and Merivale are Christchurch's villa neighborhoods. Quiet streets, good restaurants, boutique cafés. Less tourist pressure. Priced higher than Riccarton but far more relaxed than CBD hotels.

The best time for Christchurch and the South Island: October to April (New Zealand summer and autumn). Temperatures 18-25°C, longer days. December to February is peak season: high prices, tours booked out. March and April are often the best months: warm but less crowded. July to September (winter): cold, snow on nearby mountains, ski fields active.

**Who belongs here:** Couples, travelers over 40, those seeking quiet residential neighborhoods with good dining.

**Prices:** Boutique and B&B 150-280 NZD (83-154 EUR).

**Top picks:** Orari Bed and Breakfast (charming guesthouse in Merivale, very personal), Eliza's Manor Boutique Hotel (Victorian house, excellent breakfast), The George Hotel (best boutique hotel in the city, located in Merivale, very well-maintained).

## Sumner: Coast and Cliffs East of the City

Sumner is 12 kilometers east on the Pacific. The cliffs of Cave Rock, the beach, surfing. A village atmosphere that has nothing in common with the center. For day trips Sumner is ideal. For overnight stays: only if you deliberately want to be outside the city.

**Who belongs here:** Nature lovers, surfers, repeat visitors who already know Christchurch.

**Prices:** Midrange 120-220 NZD, Beachside apartments 150-280 NZD.

**Top picks:** Sumner Beach House (boutique accommodation directly on the beach), Sumner Lodge (relaxed B&B atmosphere), Esplanade Hotel Sumner (classic beach hotel, good base for south coast day trips).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How far is Queenstown from Christchurch?

Queenstown is 360 kilometers southwest. By domestic flight: 45 minutes, tickets from 60 NZD (30 EUR). By car or bus via Scenic Highway 73 and 8: about 5 to 6 hours, one of the most beautiful drives on the South Island. By InterCity bus: 7-8 hours. Flying is recommended for one-way trips. The [Queenstown guide](/en/blog/where-to-stay-queenstown) covers what comes next.

### What must you see in Christchurch?

Botanic Gardens (free, the green heart of Christchurch), Re:START Container Mall (urban history made tangible), Hagley Park (New Zealand's largest city park), Canterbury Museum (free, very good), Tram tour through the center (historic streetcar). The rebuild of Cathedral Square is still incomplete in 2026 but fascinating to observe.

### What day trips are there from Christchurch?

Akaroa (charming harbor village on a volcanic peninsula, 90 minutes, dolphin swimming), Kaikōura (whale watching, 2.5 hours north), Mount Hutt ski field (75 minutes west), Arthur's Pass National Park (mountain wilderness, 90 minutes, hiking and railway). The [Auckland guide](/en/blog/where-to-stay-auckland) gives an overview of the best island strategy for the whole country.

### How safe is Christchurch for tourists?

Christchurch is one of the safest large cities in Oceania. The only area deserving more attention: some of the not-yet-rebuilt neighborhoods east of the center after dark. The city center, Botanic Gardens, and Merivale are safe at all times.
