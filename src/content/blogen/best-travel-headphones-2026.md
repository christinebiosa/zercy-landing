Ten hours in a metal tube. Engine roar, a crying baby three rows back, the passenger beside you watching a movie at full volume. Without good headphones, a long-haul flight is an exercise in endurance. With active noise cancelling, it becomes something else entirely. Your own space, sealed off from the cabin.

The market in 2026 has never been better for travelers. Whether you want maximum ANC in an over-ear format or a compact in-ear pair that disappears into your carry-on, the options are genuinely excellent. These 7 headphones have proven themselves on real trips.

## What should you look for?

Not all headphones are good travel headphones. ANC quality is the most important factor. Active noise cancelling works best on constant broadband noise like engines and air conditioning. It handles individual voices less effectively. Chip quality is what separates the leaders from the rest. Sony and Bose have set the benchmark for years.

Battery life matters enormously on long-haul routes. Below 25 hours with ANC active is not enough. Many models now offer fast charging: 10 minutes charging for 3 hours of playback is invaluable during short layovers.

Comfort becomes critical after hour three. Soft memory-foam ear cushions, low weight, and no headband pressure are non-negotiable for over-ear models. Over-ear headphones isolate better but take up more space. If you travel [carry-on only](/en/blog/carry-on-only/), a compact in-ear model may be the smarter call.

Bluetooth multipoint connects your headphones to two devices at once. Switching between phone and laptop happens automatically, no re-pairing needed. Few people check for this feature before buying. On the road, it is one of the most practical things a pair of headphones can do. A flight adapter is no longer included with every model, but any cheap 3.5mm cable does the job.

## The 7 best travel headphones 2026

**Sony WH-1000XM5** is the most recommended travel headphone on the market. ANC performance is exceptional, sound signature warm and detailed. 30-hour battery, foldable design, multipoint Bluetooth. Anyone who has flown with them once tends not to fly without them again.

**Bose QuietComfort Ultra** sets the benchmark for comfort. The ear cushions are the softest available. ANC matches Sony in quality, with a slightly brighter sound tuning. Immersive Audio mode makes music feel noticeably more spacious at altitude.

**Apple AirPods Pro 2** are the best in-ear choice for Apple users. ANC is impressive for something this small. Transparency mode for listening to gate announcements without removing them is genuinely useful. Case-included battery reaches 30 hours. For Android users, other models make more sense.

**Apple AirPods Max** are Apple's over-ear flagship. Sound quality and ANC are at the absolute top. The weight is noticeable, but the aluminium build is a different class of premium. The included case is more protection than portability. For anyone deep in the Apple ecosystem who has the budget, these are the best headphones available.

**Sennheiser Momentum 4** is the pick for audio quality. 60 hours of battery life puts it in its own category. ANC is good but a step behind Sony and Bose. Design is clean and professional. For travelers who prioritize music quality over maximum noise isolation.

**Anker Soundcore Space Q45** is the best model under 100 euros. ANC and audio quality genuinely surprise at the price point. 50-hour battery, foldable, app-based EQ. For budget-conscious travelers who still want real noise cancelling. Pairs well with the [long-haul flight comfort guide](/en/blog/long-haul-flight-comfort-guide/).

**Bose QuietComfort Earbuds** complete the lineup as the best in-ear option from Bose. ANC is remarkably strong for true-wireless earbuds. The fit is secure and stays comfortable. For those who want Bose ANC without the over-ear format.

## Over-ear or in-ear for travel?

It depends mostly on your luggage situation. Over-ear models isolate better. The seal is stronger, battery life is longer, and soundstage is wider. For pure long-haul flights, over-ear wins almost every time.

In-ear models fit in any pocket. Easier to store, better for active travel segments. Hiking, city walking, train legs. Anyone who switches frequently between headphone situations will find in-ear more flexible. Many frequent travelers own both: an over-ear pair for flights, AirPods for everything else.

## How much should you spend?

Under 50 euros gets you ANC, but not truly effective noise cancellation. The Anker Space Q45 is the lower limit where it gets serious. The 100 to 200 euro range is the best value segment. Sennheiser Momentum 4 and Bose QC Ultra live here.

Above 300 euros you get the Sony XM5, AirPods Max, and Bose Ultra. These are no-compromise flagship models. Whether the jump from 150-euro alternatives justifies the price is personal. For frequent business travel or regular intercontinental flights: yes. For occasional travel: probably not. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which headphones have the best noise cancelling for flights?
Sony WH-1000XM5 and Bose QuietComfort Ultra deliver the strongest ANC available. Both suppress engine noise very effectively. For in-ear models, AirPods Pro 2 is the top performer. All three also work passively without power, using the physical seal alone.

### How much battery do I need for a long-haul flight?
For a 10 to 14-hour flight, 25 hours of ANC battery is the minimum. 30 hours is comfortable. Fast charging is a genuine advantage during short layovers. The Sennheiser Momentum 4 at 60 hours is built for multi-day trips without recharging.

### What is Bluetooth multipoint and why does it matter for travel?
Multipoint lets your headphones connect to both your phone and laptop at the same time. Switching between devices happens automatically or with one button press. Moving between phone music and a laptop film without re-pairing is more convenient than it sounds, especially during layovers.

### Which headphones fit best in carry-on luggage?
In-ear models like AirPods Pro 2 and Bose QC Earbuds are the most compact. For over-ear, foldable designs are essential: Sony XM5 and Bose QC Ultra both fold flat. AirPods Max are less compact in their case. For strict carry-on travel, in-ear models are the first choice.
