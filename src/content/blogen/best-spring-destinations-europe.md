Spring in Europe is the best compromise between everything. No summer tourist madness. No winter cold. Blossoms, green light, pleasant temperatures, affordable prices. The question isn't whether to go — it's where. This list shows you the most beautiful spring destinations worth visiting.

## Why Is Spring the Best Time to Travel in Europe?

March to May is the hidden sweet spot. Cheaper flights and hotels than in summer (often 30–40% less). Long daylight hours. No sweltering cities. Nature is fresh and green. And most importantly: major attractions are still enjoyable without the crush.

December shows what Europe looks like in winter. July shows what Europe looks like in summer. But April in Amsterdam or May in the Provence — that shows what Europe truly is. A continent in bloom.

## Which Spring Destinations in Europe Are the Most Beautiful?

**Keukenhof, Netherlands (March–May):** 7 million tulips across 32 hectares. The most famous flower garden in the world — for good reason. The [official Keukenhof website](https://keukenhof.nl) has tickets and opening times. Come on weekdays before 10am for the best photos without the crowds.

**Alentejo, Portugal (March–April):** Wild meadows full of poppies and chamomile. Portugal in spring is so quiet and so affordable that it's hard to believe so few people know about it. More in our [Portugal road trip guide](/en/blog/portugal-road-trip-route).

**Provence, France (April–June):** Lavender blooms in June, but cherry blossoms, almond blossoms, and green rolling hills are breathtaking from April. Gordes, Roussillon, Les Baux-de-Provence — medieval villages in a blooming landscape.

**Tuscany, Italy (April–May):** Cypress trees, vineyards, poppy fields, and rapeseed yellow. Tuscany in spring is the most classic image of Italy — and genuinely enjoyable without the summer tourist wave. Siena and San Gimignano over Florence for less crowding.

**Azores, Portugal (April–May):** Hydrangeas as living hedges, whales off the coast, green crater lakes. The Azores in spring are Europe's best-kept secret for nature lovers.

## How Much Does a Spring Europe Trip Cost?

Spring is the cheapest time to travel in Western Europe. Compared to summer, hotels often cost 25–40% less, flights are cheaper, and sights are less overwhelming.

**Sample budgets (per person, 7 days):**
- Amsterdam + Keukenhof: €700–1,000 (hotel, flight, food)
- Portugal Alentejo spring: €500–700 (usually includes a flight to Lisbon)
- Provence with rental car: €800–1,200 (flight to Marseille or Nice)
- Tuscany: €800–1,100 (flight to Florence or Pisa)

Book accommodation 4–6 weeks ahead for the best prices via [Booking.com](https://www.booking.com). Easter is always more expensive — the week before and after is cheaper.

## When Exactly Does What Bloom?

The question every spring traveler asks. As a rough guide:

| Location | What blooms | When |
|----------|-------------|------|
| Keukenhof | Tulips | Late March – mid-May |
| Portugal (Alentejo) | Poppies, chamomile | March – April |
| Cherry blossoms Europe | Cherry blossoms | Late March – mid-April |
| Tuscany | Poppies, rapeseed | April – May |
| Provence lavender | Lavender | Mid-June – July |
| Azores | Hydrangeas | April – October |

Bloom timing varies by 1–3 weeks depending on the year's weather. The [Visit Holland website](https://www.holland.com) and regional tourism sites post live bloom status updates.

## Which Lesser-Known Spring Destinations Are Worth It?

**Plitvice, Croatia (April–May):** The waterfalls and lakes of this national park without the summer crowds. Clear, deep green, almost empty in April.

**Slovenia (April–May):** Blooming apple trees in the Logarska dolina valley, turquoise Soča River, Lake Bled with spring reflections. More in our [Slovenia guide](/en/blog/slovenia-green-secret-2026).

**Northern Albania (April–May):** Albanian Alps with fresh snow on the peaks, green valleys below. Almost no tourists, extraordinary hiking routes. Combine it with the [Balkan road trip](/en/blog/balkan-road-trip-route).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When do cherry blossoms bloom in Europe?
Cherry blossoms in Europe start earlier in the south. Spain and Portugal: late February to March. Germany and Austria: late March to April. Scandinavia: May. The densest cherry blossom experience in Europe is in Bonn (old town), Wachau Valley (Lower Austria), and southern England.

### Which European cities are most beautiful in spring?
Amsterdam in spring with tulips is world-famous. But also Seville in April (Feria de Abril spring festival), Paris in May (green parks, café terraces), Lisbon in March (fresh and blue), and Budapest in April (Danube waterfront without summer crowds) are extraordinary.

### How do you avoid the crowds at spring sights?
Easter is always busy — the week before and after is quieter and cheaper. Weekdays over weekends. Early hours (before 9am) for photos. Popular spots like Keukenhof: Monday to Thursday is significantly more pleasant than Saturday.

### What clothing do you need in spring in Europe?
Layering. Mornings and evenings are cooler (8–15°C), midday can be warm (18–25°C in the south). A mid-weight jacket plus a rain layer covers almost every spring scenario. Southern Europe (Portugal, Spain, Greece) is warmer than Northern Europe — adjust your packing accordingly.
