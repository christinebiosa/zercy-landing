A honeymoon is different from any other holiday. Not because the destination must be extraordinary. But because the occasion is. Still, few trips carry as much pressure of expectations. Too expensive, too luxurious, not perfect enough?

This guide shows you how to plan a honeymoon that suits both of you, whether your budget is €3,000 or €15,000.

## What are the best honeymoon destinations?

**Maldives:** The first image that comes to mind when someone says "honeymoon." Overwater bungalows, turquoise sea, sunsets over the Indian Ocean. Why everyone goes: because it works. No cars, no noise, no city. One goal: relax and enjoy. Budget: from €4,000 per person.

**Santorini:** The European alternative to the Maldives. White buildings, blue domes, caldera views, sunsets over Oia. High season (July/August) is crowded and expensive. April/May or September/October: much quieter and 30-40% cheaper.

**Bali:** The destination for couples who want more than a beach. Ubud rice terraces, temples, spa culture and excellent villa accommodation for under €200 per night. Avoid rainy season (November to March).

**Tuscany:** For anyone who does not want long flights. Vineyards, rolling hills, medieval towns. Agritourismos offer romantic accommodation directly in the vineyard. Spring (April/May) and autumn (September/October) are ideal.

**Norway (Fjords):** For nature-oriented couples. Fjords, midnight sun, Hurtigruten coastal ship. No mass tourism. Clean, quiet, breathtaking.

**Madagascar:** Exotic, barely touristy and unlike anywhere else. For couples who really want to escape. Lemurs, tropical forests, empty beaches. Not for everyone, but for the right people, unforgettable.

## When should you book a honeymoon?

**Timing matters for two reasons:** availability and price.

Popular honeymoon resorts in the Maldives book up 6-12 months in advance, especially between Christmas and April. If you want this period, plan early.

**Off-season saves 30-40%.** Santorini in September instead of July: significantly cheaper, less crowded, same quality. Maldives in May/June (shoulder season): lowest prices, slightly windier, still beautiful.

The honeymoon does not have to happen right after the wedding. Many couples celebrate first and travel weeks or months later. This allows more planning time and often better prices.

## How much does a honeymoon cost?

**Budget: €3,000-5,000 for 2 people (7-10 days)**

Destinations: Bali, Tuscany, Greece (mainland proximity), Portugal (Alentejo). Good boutique hotels, no luxury resorts. Romantic and beautiful without Maldives prices.

**Mid-range: €5,000-8,000 for 2 people (10-14 days)**

Destinations: Santorini, South Africa (Cape Town plus wine region plus beach), Maldives entry-level package. An upgraded room, special hotel treatment, surprises are feasible.

**Luxury honeymoon: €10,000-20,000+**

Maldives overwater bungalow at a top resort, seaplane transfer, private pool, couples spa package. Or: private Botswana safari plus Zanzibar. Bali top villa with butler service.

## What makes honeymoon hotels special?

Most good hotels and resorts offer the following for registered honeymoon couples (either complimentary or as a bookable package):

Petal bath (bed or bathtub decorated with flower petals), welcome champagne, turndown service with small surprises, couples massage, sunset dinner on the beach or private terrace.

**Tip:** Always contact the hotel directly when booking and again shortly before arrival to confirm as a honeymoon couple. Even without a booked package, many hotels do something. Not saying anything means getting nothing.

Book surprise experiences in advance: private guided hike, cooking class, wine tasting at a vineyard, hot air balloon ride. Services like GetYourGuide or Viator work well. One planned experience per day, one day spontaneous.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When is the best time for a Maldives honeymoon?

December to April (dry season) is the best window: sunny, little rain, turquoise water. High season, therefore more expensive. January to March slightly cheaper than Christmas/New Year. May and October are cheaper shoulder seasons with great weather.

### What is important for honeymoon travel insurance?

Make sure the policy covers trip cancellation (including illness), lost luggage and ideally wedding cancellation too. Check that activities (diving, snorkelling, excursions) are included in the coverage.

### How far in advance should you book honeymoon hotels?

Luxury resorts in the Maldives: 6-12 months. Popular Santorini hotels in summer: 4-6 months. Bali and Tuscany: 2-4 months usually suffice. Early booking discounts are real everywhere: up to 20-30% cheaper.

### Which honeymoon destinations work on a smaller budget?

Bali (excellent villas for €100-200/night), Portugal (Alentejo, Douro Valley), Albania (Riviera), Croatia (around Dubrovnik, Hvar). Affordable flight plus romantic boutique hotel often creates more of an experience than an expensive resort at an overcrowded destination.
